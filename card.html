Axolotl

Axolotl have an astonishing ability to regenerate body organs and lost limbs.

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Average Length: 9.1in
  • Average Lifespan: Between 10 to 15 years
  • Habitat: Exclusively Mexico's Lake Xochimilco and Lake Chalco

Shrouded in mystery, and defying typical biological laws like metamorphosis, the axolotl (pronounced AX-oh-lot-ul), a type of salamander, keeps its webbed feet firmly placed in infancy throughout its life.
